Our Mission

WHY GO BLUE?

Without the big blues, humanity cannot sustain.

  • "From the air we breathe, to the food we eat, to the climate we live in, we all depend on our oceans. Today, they are more important than ever. Healthy oceans absorb carbon from the atmosphere and help reduce the impact of climate change."
    - Paul Nicklen

OXYEGN

  • Phytoplankton absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and produces more than half of the Earth's oxygen.

FOOD

  • Fisherie supply food and jobs to thousands of families across the globe. Many developing countries rely solely on the ocean for food.

CLIMATE

  • Transportation of warm water and rain from the equator to the southern and northern poles and cold water back to the tropics regulates our planet's climate.

SPECIES

  • Over two billion species call the ocean their home from the larget living animal to the smallest bacteria.

HOW WE CAN HELP

OUR PASSION

  • Beyond the fast mentioned prior, we believe in helping those who cannot help themsevles. Our oceans cannot fight for itself when we are the ones producing the damage. Pollution, emissions, run offs etc.

EXPEDITIONS

  • Our excursions allow for people, like yourselves, to advocate for and experience our oceans first-hand. You can hear us talk about how the oceans deserve our help but the excursions show you.

CAMPAIGNS

  • Along with expeditions, we need to advocate. As SeaSome grows, so does the powerful impact of our water's sufrage finally surfaces.

ORGANIZATIONS LIKE US

  • But thankfully, we are not alone. Many organizations like Mission Blue and Sea Legacy are taking major steps to work towards a more sustainable life for our oceans. We highly encourage you to chaek out these orgs and those alike to broaden your outreach and knowledge of the cause!
