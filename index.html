The Team

Rachel Waag

The founder, the techie and the jack of all trades. The success of SeaSome lies in the hard work of Rachel's dreams. Inspired by the many movements to encourage environmental conservation, her heart forever lies in the health of our Earth.

Scott Farrer

Scott began his journey in a nature photographer mentorship, capturing the ungodly tragedy that humans inhibit on our environment. Inspired by his missions, he plans to dedicate his photography to advocate for environmental wellness.

Stephanie Miller

Stephanie is a wildlife biologist specializing in Yosemite's Sierra Nevada Bighorn Sheep (Ovis canadensis sierrae). She has been successfully leading excursions since the founding of SeaSome in some of the most extravegant environments in the world.

Becca Roseman

We owe a round of applause to Becca daily. She writes grants for us and keeps SeaSome moving forward as a non-profit so that we can continue to be the voice for our oceans. She works incredibly hard to keep us above water.
