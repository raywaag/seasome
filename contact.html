FAQ's

Who can join?

SeaSome excursions are not only open to anyone with a passion for photography but also a general passion for the outdoors! This typically includes photographers and their family and/or friends. We as that anyone under 12 years stay at home for the saftey of themselves and the experience of other excursion mates.

How do I join an excursion?

You can join by going to that excursions page! Just select either the "join" or "sign up" buttons on that page and fill out the following forms. You're all set after that and we will keep you updated up until the trip date!

When are new excursions added?

The excursion locations shown have trips within the next year. They are updated accordingly!

How physically challenging are these excursions?

Depends on the location. However, our trip leaders are there to help, support and encourage the group to keep on truckin'! If you have worries about an excursion's strenuousness, send an ask on that excursion's page!

How can I cancel an excurion?

You can cancel up until 7 days before the excursion's date. In your emails, there will be options to cancel. If you have further troubles, please do no hesitate to contact us!

Can I purchase a spot in an excursion the week of?

Well, yes, but...there most likely will not be any spots left. They fill quickly especially as the trip nears. Keep an eye out for how many spots are available on the excursion's page.

What if the excursion I want is sold out?

Keep checking back as some travelers may cancel and spots could re-open. Excursions in a locations occur multiple times a year, so look into the date options and see what works best for you!
