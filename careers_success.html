TOUR GUIDE

The founder, the techie and the jack of all trades. The success of SeaSome lies in the hard work of Rachel's dreams. Inspired by the many movements to encourage environmental conservation, her heart forever lies in the health of our Earth.

Key activities

  • Manage existing relationships to engage our Collective, Swell, Compass and Tide communities.
  • Manage existing relationships to engage external donors, funders, partners and supporters.
  • Develop and execute strategies to grow new, mutually beneficial relationships across the globe.
  • Collaborate with leadership and project teams to enhance relationships.
  • Work with organizational leads to grow revenue channels and opportunities.
  • Develop, track and report on KPIs.

Expertise needed

  • 10+ years managing community, donor and/or funder relationships.

  • Working knowledge of CRM and e-commerce tools.
  • Thorough understanding of relationship tracking metrics and analyses tools.
  • Significant experience planning and managing campaigns.
  • Excellent communication, writing, collaboration, and project management skills.

RELATIONSHIP COORDINATOR

As the Relationship Manager, your primary responsibility is to manage existing relationships and cultivate new ones with individual donors, community members, corporate partners, mentors, volunteers, and foundations -- with a focus on growing these relationships so they become increasingly meaningful, valuable, and sustainable. This position will require adaptive relationship skills, interaction with high-net-worth individuals and corporate donors, and a strong, self-motivated work ethic. The ideal Relationship Manager has experience getting the 'right contributors in the right seats' by presenting opportunities to engage that are customized and specific to the prospect's goals and values, that are a match with the organization's needs, that can be sustained for the long-term, and that the organization can deliver on.
