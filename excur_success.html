About

Ready for your adventure? DIVE IN!

Our excursions can last between 3 to 10 days depending on location. You can review our upcoming excursions above.

What's included?

Food is provided on hikes and, per location, you may be granted access to local cafés. Each excursion is lead by 2 - 3 incedible trip captains (see below) who are there to help you hike, learn about your surroundings, stay safe and aware, as well as photograph some of the best locations in the world! Along with their never-ending knowledge, they have a variety of cameras and lenses including Nikon, Canon, Sony, Fujifilm and even a Hasselblad (never heard of it? Oh, just you wait!).

With this diversity of cameras, you'll have the opportunity to explore different makes, models, modes and lenses.

What to bring?

To be safe, it is reccommended you bring everything you would on a personal vacation in the wild. That is, backpacking gear, snacks, camera gear, hiking attire. We will provide tenting, camping accessaries, first aid and emergency back-up equiptment.
